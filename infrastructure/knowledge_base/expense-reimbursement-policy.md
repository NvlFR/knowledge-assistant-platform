# SOP: Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Eligible Expenses
- Client meetings and business travel (transportation, lodging, meals within per-diem).
- Work-related software/tool subscriptions approved in advance by the department head.
- Internet/phone allowance for remote employees, up to Rp 300,000/month.

## Per-Diem Rates (Domestic Travel)
| Role Level | Meals/Day | Lodging/Night |
|---|---|---|
| Staff | Rp 150,000 | Rp 500,000 |
| Manager | Rp 200,000 | Rp 750,000 |
| Head of Department+ | Rp 250,000 | Rp 1,000,000 |

## How to Submit a Reimbursement
1. Submit receipts via the Finance Portal within **14 days** of the expense date.
   Claims submitted after 30 days are not eligible for reimbursement.
2. Attach the original receipt (photo or PDF) and select the correct expense category.
3. Direct manager approval is required for all claims; claims above Rp 5,000,000
   additionally require Finance Head approval.
4. Approved reimbursements are paid out in the next payroll cycle (twice monthly).

## Non-Reimbursable Items
Alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment budget), personal entertainment,
fines/penalties, and any expense without a valid receipt.
