# SOP: New Employee Onboarding

## Before Day 1
- IT provisions a company laptop and email account.
- HR sends the offer letter, employment contract, and first-day schedule.
- Direct manager assigns an onboarding buddy.

## Day 1 Checklist
1. HR orientation session (company policies, benefits, code of conduct).
2. IT setup session: VPN access, internal tools (Slack, Jira, Confluence), and this
   AI Assistant Platform account creation.
3. Meet the team and onboarding buddy.
4. Access request for department-specific systems (e.g., CRM for Sales, AWS for
   Engineering) is submitted by the manager on Day 1 and typically approved within
   2 working days.

## First 30 Days
- Complete mandatory compliance training (Data Privacy, Information Security).
- Weekly 1:1 with direct manager to track ramp-up progress.
- 30-day review with HR and direct manager.

## Offboarding Note
For offboarding procedures, see `offboarding-sop.md`. All company assets (laptop, access
badge) must be returned on the employee's last working day.
