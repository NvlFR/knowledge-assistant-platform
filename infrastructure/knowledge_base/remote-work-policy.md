# SOP: Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

## Eligibility
Employees who have completed their 3-month probation period and whose role does not
require fixed on-site presence (e.g. office facilities, hardware lab work) are eligible
for hybrid work.

## Work Arrangement
- Standard hybrid schedule: minimum **3 days per week in the office**, remaining days
  remote, coordinated with the direct manager.
- Fully remote arrangements require Head of Department approval and are reviewed
  quarterly.
- Core collaboration hours are **10:00–16:00 local time**, during which employees should
  be reachable regardless of work location.

## Equipment & Expenses
- Company provides a laptop; a one-time home-office setup allowance of Rp 2,000,000 is
  available for eligible remote employees (see `expense-reimbursement-policy.md` for the
  monthly internet/phone allowance).

## Security Requirements for Remote Work
- Must connect to internal systems via company VPN.
- Public Wi-Fi (e.g. cafes, co-working spaces) requires VPN to be active at all times;
  see `data-security-policy.md` for the broader data classification and access rules.
- Screens must be locked when stepping away, even at home.

## Requesting a Schedule Change
Submit a request via the HR Portal at least 1 week in advance; manager approval is
required before the new arrangement takes effect.
