# SOP: Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to **12 days of annual leave** per calendar year,
accrued monthly (1 day/month). Unused leave can be carried over to the next year up to a
maximum of 6 days; anything beyond that is forfeited on December 31st.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to sick leave with a valid doctor's note for absences longer than
1 day. Sick leave of 1 day does not require documentation but must be reported to your
direct manager before 9 AM on the day of absence.

## How to Request Leave
1. Submit a leave request via the Internal HR Portal at least 3 working days in advance
   (except for emergencies).
2. Your direct manager must approve the request before it is considered final.
3. HR will send a confirmation email once approved.

## Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be granted for a maximum of 30 days per year, subject to approval from
both the direct manager and the Head of Department.
